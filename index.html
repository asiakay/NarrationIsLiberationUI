 Mission

“Narration is Liberation” is a biweekly (twice a month) spoken word residency whose goal is, in a sustainable fashion, to support artists in their efforts to give voice to silenced parts of the self or community.

We aim to spark conversation and action leading liberation from oppression, be it personal/intrapsychic or global/geopolitical.
